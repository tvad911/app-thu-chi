# Privacy Policy for Thu Chi (Personal Finance Manager)

**Last updated: March 08, 2026**

Thank you for choosing to use **Thu Chi**, a personal finance management application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

This Privacy Policy applies to all information collected through our App.

## 1. Information We Do Not Collect
We believe your financial data is deeply personal. **Thu Chi is an offline-first application**. 
- We do not operate any servers to collect, store, or process your financial transactions, account balances, or any personal data.
- All data entered into the App is stored locally on your device using a local SQLite database.

## 2. Cloud Synchronization (Google Drive Integration)
The App offers an optional feature to synchronize your database backup and attachments (like receipt images) to your personal **Google Drive**.

When you choose to enable Google Drive sync:
- The App requests read and write access strictly limited to the files it creates (`https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file` scope).
- The App does not have access to any other files or folders in your Google Drive.
- Your data is directly transferred between your device and Google's servers. We do not intercept, route, or have any access to this data flow.
- The use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the [Google API Services User Data Policy](https://developers.google.com/terms/api-services-user-data-policy), including the Limited Use requirements.

## 3. Local Authentication
The App may utilize your device's built-in local authentication features (like PIN, Face ID, or Fingerprint) to secure your data. This authentication happens entirely on your device. We do not collect or transmit your biometrics or PIN codes.

## 4. Third-Party Services
The App does not use third-party analytics or tracking SDKs that send your app usage data to external marketing companies.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this privacy policy frequently to be informed of how we are protecting your information.

If you have questions or comments about this policy, you may contact the developer by opening an issue on our GitHub repository:
